# Narikootam Run! 🐾

A cooperative puzzle platformer game built with Phaser 3. Work together with up to 4 players to collect keys, avoid hazards, and reach the door!

## 🎮 Game Features

- **1-4 Player Co-op**: Play solo or with friends (local multiplayer)
- **10 Unique Characters**: Choose from Sanju, Sabbu, Kurkure, Mapla, Mama, Kk, Muong, Chozha, Ash, and Kallakurichi
- **20 Levels**: 5 levels for each player count (1p, 2p, 3p, 4p)
- **Player Stacking**: Stack players on top of each other to reach high places
- **Moving Platforms**: Navigate challenging moving platform sections
- **Hazards**: Avoid spikes and other dangers

## 🎯 How to Play

### Controls

**1 Player Mode:**
- Arrow Keys: ← ↑ → (Move and Jump)

**2 Player Mode:**
- Player 1: A W D (Move and Jump)
- Player 2: ← ↑ → (Move and Jump)

**3 Player Mode:**
- Player 1: A W D
- Player 2: ← ↑ →
- Player 3: J I L

**4 Player Mode:**
- Player 1: A W D
- Player 2: ← ↑ →
- Player 3: J I L
- Player 4: F T H

**Universal Controls:**
- ESC: Pause game
- CTRL+F: Toggle fullscreen

### Objective

1. Collect the key (yellow)
2. Reach the door (green)
3. All players must reach the door to win!

## 🚀 Deployment

3. Deploy!
